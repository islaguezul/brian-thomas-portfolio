James Liu reviewed Sony WH-1000XM5
5 hours ago
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

The noise canceling on these is genuinely next-level. I wore them on a cross-country flight and couldn't hear the engines at all. Sound quality is warm and detailed — bass hits without being muddy. My only gripe is the touch controls can be finicky in cold weather. Comfort-wise, I can wear these for 6+ hours with no fatigue. If you commute or travel, these are a no-brainer.

Sarah Reeves reviewed Eagle Creek Trail
Yesterday
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Finally got to hike Eagle Creek after the reopening and it absolutely lives up to the hype. Punchbowl Falls is stunning — the turquoise pool looks almost unreal. Trail is well-maintained but narrow in spots, so trekking poles help. We went on a Tuesday morning and mostly had it to ourselves. Pack layers; the canyon stays cool even in summer.
